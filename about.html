We offer both in-studio detailing and convenient mobile services, ensuring that your vehicle receives expert care no matter where you are. Our Mission

At AutoPro, our mission is to provide exceptional, high-end detailing services that restore every vehicle to showroom or OEM condition. We are committed to combining precision techniques, eco-friendly products, and a customer-first approach to ensure every client enjoys a seamless and satisfying experience. Whether at our studio or through our mobile unit, we aim to deliver convenience without compromising quality.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the most trusted and innovative car detailing business in South Africa. We aspire to set the industry standard for excellence, expand our services nationwide, and introduce cutting-edge detailing technologies and protective solutions. Ultimately, we aim to inspire car enthusiasts and everyday drivers alike to preserve the beauty, value, and longevity of their vehicles.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We treat every car as our own and with the atmost care.
  • Trust: Building long-lasting relationships through honesty and reliability.
  • Innovation: Embracing the latest products and techniques in detailing.
  • Passion: A love for cars drives everything we do.
