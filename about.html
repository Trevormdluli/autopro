About

AutoPro is a car detailing business that was established in 2020. It operates in bugersfort and the surrounding areas. The business has both an in studio detailing and a mobile service. AutoPro is known for high end services and has accumulated a variety of clients from individual car owners to private car collectors as well as dealerships.

Mission

To provide high end services to our clients and restore their cars to OEM condition

Vision

To be the leading car detailing business while providing innovative services
